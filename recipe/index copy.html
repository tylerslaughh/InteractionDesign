How to make a Breakfast Smoothie

Description

A great smoothie to keep you energized throughout the day!

Ingredients

  • Frozen berries of your choosing
  • 1 Spoonful of Almond Butter
  • A splash of Oat Milk
  • 2 Cups of Spinach
  • 1 Spoonful of Vanilla Protein Powder
  • A Dash of Cinnamon
Instructions
  • To start the recipe, begin by adding all of the dry ingredients. I start with the spinach then the protein powder then the almond butter, cinnamon and oat milk. Blend these ingredients together. You will get a watery substance. Finally, add the frozen fruit. You add the fruit last to protect the blades of your blender. Once it is all blended, pour into a glass and enjoy:)
